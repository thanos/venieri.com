The Last Conflict

May 19, 2011

Stux Gallery is pleased to present a retrospective exhibition of the work of Lydia Venieri. The Last Conflict features Venieri’s new series of satin digital color photographs Planetic Exodus and highlights of her prolific oeuvre including works from the magical forest of Hibernation, The Dolphin Conspiracy video and a sculptural installation created with her bubble sculptures, fauna and tree debris.
