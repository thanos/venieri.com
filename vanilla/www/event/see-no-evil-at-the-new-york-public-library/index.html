  • Virtual World

Every day images of war, through the media, try to penetrate our reality. And every day these images freeze before touching our reality leaving a wretched ghost of pain to haunt us. It is this haunted look (not the ghost) I have tried to catch within the dolls eyes. This look hosts different feelings like fear or revolt, despair or denial, compassion or immunity. The beauty of the dolls and violence reflected in their eyes capture the invisible wall which separates the co-existence of these two worlds.
