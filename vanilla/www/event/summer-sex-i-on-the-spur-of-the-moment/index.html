Lichtundfire’s “SUMMER SEX: I – On the Spur of the Moment“

With Fredi Brodmann, Sheila Elias, Augustus Goertz, Linda Griggs, Don Keene, Robert Mango, Mary Ann Moy, Alan Neider, Guy Stanley Philoche, Larry Rivers, Piril Seker, Bob Stanley, Lydia Venieri.

Exhibition Dates: July 11 – August 4, 2017

Opening Reception: Wednesday, July 12, 2017 from 5:30 to 8:30 PM

Summer Sex is easy, casual, often without commitment, on the Spur of The Moment, makes unlikely partners, but is often the most enjoyable, memorable sex you had in a long time.

This is the first of two consecutive summer group exhibitions (Summer Sex I & II) at Lichtundfire, bringing together very different work in all media, from figuration to abstraction and every thing in-between. Deliberately creating a new narrative with at first sight unlikely partners or with works with obvious connotations, the exhibition seeks to engage the viewer with the familiar in a new, direct and unfamiliar way.

The show will accompanied by a catalog with an essay by Jonathan Goodman.
